Welcome to StudentsWithoutHunger

Here we want to spread awareness about the issue of food insecurity

Those at the vanguard in the fight!
Swipe Out Hunger is a nonprofit whose sole goal is to provide food to students in need. The organization's impact on colleges all around the country is very significant and has managed to improve the lives of many. Here at StudentsWithoutHunger, we want to promote this great charity, so it can do even more good in the future.

Swipe Out Hunger partners with college campuses nationwide to end student hunger.

Progress

SWH x SOH

Now or Never!

Students all around the country need support more than ever, so they can continue their education and leave poverty. Please consider supporting charities like Swipe Out Hunger, so that they can support our youth in these moments of need.

How effective is this support?

Comments from those that received the support from Swipe Out Hunger are overwhelmingly positive and the numbers show that it is having a positive impact on both students and its communities.

About

StudentsWithoutHunger was created by a small group of students at SCC with the purpose of promoting the charity Swipe Out Hunger and informing the public about the issue of students without food at colleges all around the United States of America.

StudentsWithoutHunger and those involved with the project haven't been compensated at any point and in any way by Swipe Out Hunger for this work.
